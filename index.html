Ph of Avocado

It is slightly Acidic. It's Ph value ranges from 6.27-6.58(Acc to this Source)

Nutrients in Avocado

  • Per 100gram of Avocado
  • 160 calories
  • 14.7 g of fat
  • 8.5 g of carbohydrates
  • 6.7 g of fiber
  • less than 1 g of sugar.

Taxonomy of Avocados

The common taxonomy of avocado is Persea americana
Others are Persea edulis,
Persea floccosa,
Persea gigantea,
Persea gratissima.
